Biography

Dr. Kamal Kumar Paul leads the research initiatives at the Institute of Low-Dimensional Quantum Materials. His work bridges the gap between fundamental solid-state physics and applied optoelectronics. With a deep focus on the synthesis and characterization of two-dimensional transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs), Dr. Paul's research group aims to push the boundaries of next-generation photovoltaic devices, including high-efficiency hot carrier solar cells and advanced interfacial energy systems.
