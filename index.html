Research Areas

Two-Dimensional TMDs

Exploring the unique electronic and optical properties of transition metal dichalcogenides (e.g., MoS2) and their van der Waals heterostructures for ultra-thin device integration.

Hot Carrier Solar Cells

Investigating energy-selective contacts and advanced architectures to capture high-energy charge carriers, pushing photovoltaic efficiency beyond the Shockley-Queisser limit.

Optoelectronics

Fabrication of opoelectronic devices
